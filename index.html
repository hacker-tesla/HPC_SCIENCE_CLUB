Take my salam and welcome everyone in Hamdard Public College Science Club. HPC Science Club give you the best opportunity even online. ” It is a small step for me, but a great leap for mankind.” Neil Armstrong,the first man to set foot on the Moon, said it when he landed on the moon. I think these words are relevant as we embark on simple but new programmes under the auspices of the science Club because they go a long way in the development of scientific taste, knowledge and research in students. Modern science has been hailed everywhere as the symbol of the status, liberty and power. When we analyze the gradual development of science and technology, it raises before us several questions about the identity and uniqueness of mankind. It is a boon or bane? It will definitely be a bane for the society if it is not channelised properly. Nowadays, environmental pollution is a hotly discussed issue in the social and political scenario of the world. These issues are mere symbols which show as in a mirror what we have done in the past. This is the place where students have to take responsibility in order to create awareness in the society and be the torch bearers for the forth coming generations.
