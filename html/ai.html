Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intellignece means it can control your computer by your command, and there's no need to click or type something. It can control your computer by your voice command.

A.I. means Artificial Intelligence. Now the question is how A.I. works?
A.I. is basically a sum of program. Each program contain an command. For make an A.I. first you need to add speech recognition library, because your A.I. can recognige our voice and command. Then you need to add one after another command and makes your A.I. powerful.

Next question is what can we do by A.I. ? Well by using A.I. you can control your computer without touching. Not only control your computer you can control anything you want in your computer, you can make an A.I. for Research, for solving Matmatical Equation and so on.
